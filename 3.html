There are lots of occasions when we need to use lists. HTML provides us with three different types:

  • Ordered lists(Numbered lists) are lists where each item in the list is numbered. For example, the list might be a set of steps for a recipe that must be performed in order, or a legal contract where each point needs to be identified by a section number
  • Unordered lists(Bullet lists) are lists that begin with a bullet point (rather than characters that indicate order).
  • Definition lists(Definition lists) are made up of a set of terms along with the definitions for each of those terms

Here is a simple and versatile recipe for a Vegetable Mayonnaise Sandwich, a quick, creamy, and healthy option for a snack or light meal. It can be enjoyed as a cold sandwich or lightly toasted/grilled.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or multigrain)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise (or hung curd/yogurt for a healthier option)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped capsicum (bell pepper)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped carrot (or shredded)
  • 2 tbsp sweet corn (boiled, optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • Butter or oil for toasting (optional)

Method

  1. Prepare the filling: In a mixing bowl, combine the finely chopped onion, capsicum, carrot, and boiled sweet corn.
  2. Add seasoning: Add salt and black pepper powder to the vegetables.
  3. Mix with mayonnaise: Add the mayonnaise to the vegetables and mix everything well until the vegetables are evenly coated.
  4. Assemble the sandwich:
    1. Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each bread slice (this helps prevent sogginess).
    2. Spoon a generous amount of the vegetable-mayonnaise mixture onto one slice of bread.
    3. Cover with the second slice of bread.
  5. Serve:
    1. Cold sandwich: Slice immediately into half (triangles or squares) and serve.
    2. Toasted/Grilled sandwich: Spread a little butter on the outside of the sandwich and toast on a tawa (skillet), in a sandwich maker, or a panini press until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
